# BTW Session

**Bridge to Agential Care** — A prototype for safely-scoped AI therapeutic agents.

## Vision

Growing an AI agential therapist that:
- Safely scopes to meet the needs of clients
- Centers safest best practices for relational space with AI
- Maintains clear boundaries and escalation protocols

## Three Core Experiences

1. **Triadic Alliance** — Collaborative model where clinician, AI, and client work as partners with configurable autonomy levels

2. **Standalone Ethical Agent** — Independent AI with built-in guardrails, transparent scope limits, and human escalation paths

3. **Relational Attunement Lab** — Attachment-aware responses that adapt to different relational patterns
